03 : About me

Purpose

In my past work experiences, I realized that I have limited growth in my career. In order to advance my career development, I have set a career path for myself, which is to possess a job that requires in-demand skills and motivates me to learn and grow continuously in a rapidly changing world.

Motivation

In a typical day,I read a book about the future of the economy and employment collapse in 2030 by Inoue Tomohisa.I have noticed that AI will be a big trend in the upcoming years.This has inspired me to open my journey to start coding and pursuing a career goal in the Technology field.

Action

I started to learn coding in 2021.The process of learning is actually crucial for me,yet simultaneously,my brain gets to exercise and constantly deliberate on how to overcome an error by googling,reading the technical documentation,and learning to solve problems by myself before seeking help.

Pursuit

Since then,I've set my career path as a Front-end developer.I would like to combine everything I have learned so far to provide a profound,terrific,satisfying user experience to my future clients.In the meantime,taking learning as a choice and learning to learn in my daily practice.
